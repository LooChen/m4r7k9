A Note Before We Begin

60% complete

I’m writing the following for friends and family in an attempt to share the many insights that have come to me through meditation. All of these ideas are out there already in some shape or form, but this particular configuration, this collective “system,” is how they came and continue to sit with me.

I’m writing with the help of an LLM, in this case Claude, so many of the words won’t be mine. But the point isn’t to impress you with my writing (trust me, you wouldn’t be 😊) but rather to extract the concepts and transfer them as my mind holds them. In some ways having an LLM for this is perfect, both in timing and in function. Meditative insights arrive at levels below language, and every attempt to communicate them passes through cultural, personal, and linguistic filters that shape them on the way out. An LLM can grasp the concepts and reorganize them in whatever form suits you. You could even ask it to rewrite any of this in a style you find more credible. As you’ll come to find out, that may matter more than you’d expect.

It’s probably worth saying a couple things upfront about meditative insights specifically, how they work and why they’re a little different from how we typically come to know things.

For those already versed in Buddhism or other philosophical frameworks, there’s also some relevant background on why I’m not just using or restating those. The short version is that these insights came to me independently, before I explored the details of those frameworks.

I should say, and will probably repeat, that I have no formal education, research, PhD, or credentials in any of this. Only experiential insight. Hopefully any validation of these concepts comes from your own real-world experience, from testing these ideas out for yourself. This is available to everyone.

Besides, when it comes to deeper wisdom, any analytical concepts are just pointers to the experience, which you’ll need to undertake yourself. My hope is that this is just another pointer, encouraging you toward the experiencing.
