Dissolving the Beliefs

Placeholder
Chapter overview

The second mode. Rather than rearranging the world to suit the filter, loosen the filter itself. The nihilism fear addressed directly: dissolving beliefs doesn’t produce flatness. It produces a different quality of engagement — present without stake. The belief dissolves when you see it clearly enough. What remains is the color and weight of experience without the oscillation attached.
