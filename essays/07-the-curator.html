Part II — The Curator · Chapter 7

The Curator

Coming soon
Coming from Chapter 6 Part I showed the machine: a filter built to track status, running an addiction loop below the level of conscious choice. Part II opens with a new question. The filter doesn’t just react passively to events — it actively builds something. Over time, the accumulated reactions, the kept moments and discarded ones, construct an identity. This chapter names the mechanism that does that building: the curator.
Chapter overview

The ego isn’t just a filter that colors experience. It’s also an archivist — actively maintaining a coherent story about who you are. The curator is the part of the mechanism that does this work. It keeps the memories that confirm your self-image and lets the contradicting ones fade.

The curator’s selection criterion isn’t truth or accuracy. It’s status signal. This sets up Chapter 8 directly: if the curator checks credentials before content, what happens to wisdom that arrives without credentials?
